A little bit about me...

I enjoy diving into new and unfamiliar terrain. There’s something invigorating about researching and understanding the qualitative and quantitative data and being able to synthesize that into insights to tackle challenges. A moment comes where things begin to click and the pieces all start to fall in line - a moment where you can begin to see how much further the road really goes. Thats the moment I strive for with every new experience. I believe in learning how to do things myself and to push myself to learn what I need to in order to solve what I set my mind to.

Right now I’m interested in tackling problems that can make everyday tasks easier. Whether thats improving a user’s experience or applying machine intelligence to solve problems or preferably some combination of the two. I’m currently actively seeking new product roles.

How I work:

I have a DIY mentality. After aligning on what we're trying to do, I like to go as far as I can, as quickly as I can. Then I like to work with others to share and combine ideas to see how we can evolve to create something greater than the individual components. I generally take the following steps:

  • Research
  • Design
  • Plan
  • Execute
  • Analyze
  • Refine
  • Iterate
